Groups vs. Tags

Groups

Each node corresponds to an exclusive category:
  • A1
  • - B1
  • - - C1
  • - - C2
  • - - C3
  • - B2
  • - B3
  • - - C4
  • - - C5 
A1 -+- B1 -+- C1
    |      +- C2
    |      \- C3
    +- B2
    \- B3 -+- C4
           \- C5

Tags

Each node corresponds to a set of tags:
  • A, C, E
  • - A
  • - - A, D
  • - - A, D
  • - - A, D
  • . B
  • - C 
A,C,E -+- A -+- A,D
       |     +- A,D
       |     \- A,D
       |  B
       |
       \- C
